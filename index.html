About Us

Our Quality of work and attention to detail has given us the ability to thrive from one drilling unit in the 80's to a fleet of 10 and counting!

To keep up with today's oil and gas standards, Rathole Drilling continues to raise the bar of safety and thrives on being the absolute highest quality services company in all of Texas.
